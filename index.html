+

It all depends on the niche and the value of the product. If the product to 130$ - advertising budget of 6-13 $ per day. If over, then the advertising budget is allocated from 20 $.

+

Every month I report my results (I can send them once a week if needed), and we discuss whether everything is in order, whether we are increasing or decreasing the budget. I am in touch with you every day if you have any questions.
